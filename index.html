About Sauti Sol

Sauti Sol are a Kenyan afro-pop band formed in Nairobi, Kenya by vocalists Bien-Aimé Baraza, Willis Chimano and Savara Mudigi in 2005. Initially an a cappella group, guitarist Polycarp Otieno joined before they named themselves Sauti.The actual reason why this band is highly recommended as the best and favourite band in Kenya is because its songs are so patriotic and their songs has powerful and encouraging songs that empowers youths in the curtent society.
